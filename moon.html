    Transportation is Key
    1. History

  • Founded in 1788, 12 years after the year of independence of the United States.

  • Throughout the 19th and 20th centuries, it has seen great growth in its economy in the construction of railroads and bridges

  • Also known in the WWII era as a major center of manufacturing.

    2. Proximity to Transportation Hub

  • Contains the Pittsburgh International Airport, allowing for great transport opportunities

  • Boasts 94% high school graduation rate in its population of 25,000

  • Also demonstrates a high rate of higher education, with 45% of the population holding a bachelor's degree or higher.

  • Its median income is $75,000, much higher than the national average.
