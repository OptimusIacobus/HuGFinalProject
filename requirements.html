    • General Requirements for a Site

    (What is needed for a Tech Company Headquarter)

    There are several key requirements that a site needs in order to be optimal for a major technology company's headquarters.
    1.Space

  • In the area, there has to be existing buildings of at least 500,000 square feet, and site space of at max 8 million square feet.

    2.Location

  • The HQ2 has to be located 30 miles from a population center, and 45 minutes to the airport.

    3.Costs

  • The site should have low operating costs and a business friendly environment – competition and growth with other businesses – agglomeration is optimal

    4.Transportation

  • The site should have public transportation, accessibility to the CBD and the international airport

    5.Education

  • The site should have a highly educated labor force fit for Amazon.

    6.Culture

  • The site should have a diverse, desirable community to live in, area should include many cultures.
